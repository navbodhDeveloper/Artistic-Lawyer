About the Artist

Radhika Rungta is a passionate and imaginative painter whose work draws the viewer into a world of emotion, colour and storytelling. Her journey began with a fascination for light and landscape—transforming everyday moments into striking compositions. Through bold brush-strokes and a rich palette, Radhika captures the interplay between form and feeling, inviting an intimate connection with each viewer. Her artistic vision centres on the harmony between nature, human experience and artistic expression—each piece growing out of lived reflection and creative discovery. With a dedication to craft, authenticity and visual poetry, Radhika brings a unique voice to the art world, inviting you to explore, engage and be inspired.
